SOFLE RGB

$75.00

  • DEFAULT
  • DEFAULT


1

The latest revision in the Sofle family! This version supports per-key and underglow LEDs. The per-key LEDs are 3535 mini-e, making them easier to solder. The underglow are 5050 LEDs.

Each switch hole has a Kailh MX socket, making it easy for you to swap out the switches whenever you please!

It comes with encoders for both sides and knobs as well. The knobs included will likely be different to the ones pictured. I will have other options later.

Everything included

  • 60 MX Kailh sockets
  • 60 SMD diodes
  • 75 3535 mini-e LEDs
  • 2 encoders
  • 2 encoder knobs
  • 2 TRRS jacks
  • 2 reset buttons
  • FR-4 plates acrylic OLED covers
  • Screws standoffs
  • PCBs (left right)

Things you’ll need to add / purchase separately

  • 2 microcontrollers — Pro Micro, nice!nano, or orelite c
  • 2 OLEDs (optional)
  • 1 TRRS cable
  • 1 USB-C / Micro-USB cable to connect to your computer
  • 58 MX switches
